Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

NamasteeWanderrlust ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what personal data we collect, how we use it, and the rights you have over that data when you visit namasteewanderrlust.com or book a group trip with us.

1. What we collect

  • Account info: name, email, phone number — provided when you sign up or book a trip.
  • Booking info: trip selection, package, travel dates, co-traveller names.
  • Payment info: handled by Razorpay. We never store card numbers or bank details.
  • Usage data: pages viewed, device/browser, IP address — collected via cookies & analytics.

2. How we use it

To process bookings, send trip confirmations & itineraries, provide customer support, improve the site, and occasionally share relevant new trips (you can opt out any time).

3. Who we share it with

Only the third parties required to operate the service — Razorpay (payments), Resend/Supabase (transactional emails & auth), Vercel (hosting), Google Analytics (anonymised usage). We never sell your data.

4. Cookies

We use essential cookies for login/session and analytics cookies to understand traffic. You can disable cookies in your browser — some site features may stop working.

5. Your rights

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data at any time. Email us at namasteewanderrlust@gmail.com.

6. Data retention

Booking and financial records are retained for 7 years to meet Indian tax & regulatory requirements. Marketing contacts are kept until you unsubscribe.

7. Contact
