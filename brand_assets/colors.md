# NamasteeWanderrlust Brand Color System

## Brand
- **Name:** NamasteeWanderrlust
- **Tagline:** Travel the World with Your Favorite Influencer

## Primary Colors
- Sunset Orange: #D4773B — Primary CTAs, logo accent, hover states
- Golden Amber: #D4A843 — Highlights, badges, pricing, luxury accents
- Deep Black: #1A1A1A — Navbar, footer, dark hero overlays, headings
- Warm Beige: #EDE8DC — Main page background, card backgrounds

## Secondary Colors
- Teal Mountain: #2A7B8E — Secondary buttons, links, info badges
- Forest Green: #3B6B3A — Success states, nature sections, trip tags
- Deep Teal: #1D5B6B — Dark accent for teal hover states
- Sky Blue: #5BA4C9 — Light info backgrounds, secondary links

## Accent Colors
- Lotus Pink: #C75B7A — Special highlights, limited-time badges
- Terracotta: #B85C38 — Warm secondary accent, earthy callouts
- Sand: #C4A97D — Muted gold for borders, dividers

## Neutrals & Text
- Off White: #F5F0E8 — Body background, light card fills
- Cream Light: #FAF7F2 — Lightest background
- Warm Brown: #2C2418 — Primary body text (NOT pure black)
- Muted Brown: #6B6356 — Secondary text, captions
- Pure White: #FFFFFF — Card surfaces, text on dark backgrounds

## Rules
- NEVER use #000000 for text — always #2C2418
- NEVER use #FFFFFF for backgrounds — always #F5F0E8 or #EDE8DC
- Card shadows: rgba(44, 36, 24, 0.06), hover: rgba(44, 36, 24, 0.12)
- Hero overlays: linear-gradient(to top, rgba(26,26,26,0.7), transparent)
- Primary CTA: bg #D4773B, text white, hover darken 10%
- Secondary CTA: bg #D4A843, text #1A1A1A
- Vibe: WARM, EARTHY, PREMIUM — luxury travel magazine
