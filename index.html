Our Story

Founded by Parull & Anagh

✦

Parull Chaudhary

Co-Founder · Storyteller · Host

Anagh Bharat

Co-Founder · Strategist · Curator

Meet the dynamic duo behind Namastee Wanderrlust — Parull Chaudhary and Anagh Bharat, two powerhouses who turned their passion into a vibrant travel movement! Parull brings her on-screen charisma and storytelling magic from her acting journey and her experience as a host with Aaj Tak. She knows how to capture moments, engage audiences, and turn every destination into a story worth telling. Whether it's in front of the camera or behind the scenes, her energy is infectious and absolutely travel-ready!

On the other side, Anagh Bharat adds the perfect blend of strategy and execution, coming from a strong pharmaceutical marketing background. He's the brain that transforms ideas into scalable experiences, ensuring every trip is not just fun but flawlessly curated. Together, they sparked the exciting concept of traveling with influencers — blending adventure, content, and community into one unforgettable experience. With their combined expertise and unstoppable enthusiasm, they're not just planning trips... they're creating journeys people never want to end!
