Why Munnar keeps drawing travellers back

Tucked into the Western Ghats of Kerala at 1,600 m elevation, Munnar is a cloud-kissed hill town famous for rolling tea estates, waterfalls, spice plantations and cool monsoon-kissed air. It's easily the most photogenic hill station in South India, and one of the most accessible from Bengaluru, Kochi and Chennai.
