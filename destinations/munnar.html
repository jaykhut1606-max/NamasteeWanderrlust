Day-by-Day

Your 4-day Munnar itinerary

From Cochin arrival to tea museum farewell — here's exactly how your trip unfolds.

Day

1

Apr 29 · Wednesday

Cochin arrival & welcome to Munnar

Land at Cochin (COK) where our team meets you with a placard. Settle into our air-conditioned traveller for the scenic 4-hour drive up into the Western Ghats, with a coffee & snacks stop. Check in, freshen up, and meet the group at a tea-plantation welcome dinner under the stars.

  • Airport pickup at COK
  • Drive Cochin → Munnar
  • Check-in & welcome kit
  • Welcome dinner & introductions

Meals included: Welcome dinner

Day

2

Apr 30 · Thursday

The classic Munnar loop

A full day covering the sights that put Munnar on the map. Boating at Mattupetty Dam, clap-back echoes at Echo Point, a quiet shikara at Kundala Lake, and a group reel at Top Station as the sun sets over the Ghats.

  • Mattupetty Dam boating
  • Echo Point photo stop
  • Kundala Lake shikara
  • Top Station sunset

Meals included: Breakfast + Group Dinner

Day

3

May 1 · Friday

Wildlife, spice & waterfalls

Early start to beat the queue at Eravikulam National Park — home to the endangered Nilgiri tahr and India's second-tallest peak, Anamudi. Afternoon at a working spice garden (cardamom, vanilla, black pepper) followed by cooling off at the layered Attukal Waterfalls.

  • Eravikulam National Park
  • Spice garden walking tour
  • Attukal Waterfalls
  • Candlelit group dinner

Meals included: Breakfast + Group Dinner

Day

4

May 2 · Saturday

Tea museum & Cochin send-off

Last morning in the hills — a slow walk through the estate gardens, then a guided tour of the Tata Tea Museum to see how that chai in your cup actually gets made. Drive back to Cochin with afternoon drops at the airport or city hotels.

  • Morning tea garden walk
  • Tata Tea Museum visit
  • Drive Munnar → Cochin
  • Airport drop (flights 6 PM+)

Meals included: Breakfast

Frequently Asked

Everything travellers ask us

What is included in the Munnar 4-day group trip?

The ₹30,010 package covers 3 nights' twin-sharing accommodation at tea estate properties, daily breakfast and 2 dinners, all internal transport, all sightseeing entry fees, an English-speaking Indian host (Parull Chaudhry), a welcome kit and 24/7 WhatsApp support. Flights to Cochin, lunches and personal expenses are additional.

How do I reach Munnar for this trip?

You fly into Cochin International Airport (COK), where our team receives you at the arrival gate with a name placard. From Cochin to Munnar is a scenic 4-hour drive (110 km) with a coffee-break stop along the way. Flights to Cochin are not included in the package — we recommend booking at least 4-6 weeks ahead.

Who is Parull Chaudhry, our trip host?

Parull Chaudhry is the Co-Founder of NamasteeWanderrlust and a popular Indian travel and lifestyle content creator. She personally hosts our Munnar trips — from welcome dinners to group reels — making sure every traveller feels looked after. Past group-trip members describe her as warm, fun and genuinely invested in their experience.

Is this trip good for solo travellers?

Absolutely — roughly 40% of our travellers book solo, and we intentionally keep groups small (max 15) so you feel like part of a family rather than a faceless tour. Twin sharing is available at no single supplement if you're open to sharing with another solo traveller of the same gender. Solo private rooms are ₹8,000 extra.

What is the cancellation policy?

Cancellations 30+ days before departure: 75% refund of deposit. 15-30 days before: 50%. Less than 15 days: no refund. We recommend purchasing travel insurance for flight cancellations or emergencies. Our team is usually flexible with date swaps if you cancel because of illness (medical certificate required).

Can I customise the trip or add extra days?

Group trips follow a fixed itinerary for logistics, but we're happy to recommend and book add-ons like an extra day in Alleppey's backwaters, a night at a Thekkady wildlife resort or a Cochin heritage tour before/after your trip. Share your plans at booking and we'll arrange transfers and stays at group rates.
